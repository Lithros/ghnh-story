Great Hearts, Noble Hearts
gameplay / courting
Jules Lavelle 8/11/2020 1:00 PM
As a weekly action, players may attempt to court a mistress. Courting costs money, and its difficulty depends on the relative social status between the player and the prospective mistress. Review the Mistress Courting Table to see the die roll needed for successful courting. Characters may pay the cost of courting again, as many times as necessary, to make the roll easier by one step, unless the mistress is Wealthy. Once a mistress has been successfully courted, the character must pay the courting cost in upkeep each month (again, excepting Wealthy mistresses, who pay the character if his social level is lower). Mistresses, like bawdyhouses, fulfill the need for female companionship, either by visiting them, or bringing them to a club. Having a mistress confers one status point per month. If the mistress is of a higher social level, extra status will be earned. If the mistress is Beautiful, extra status will be earned. Mistresses provide influence once per year to their lovers based on the mistress's social level, and once again if they are Influential. (edited)
